Our Products

  • Whole Milk — Our classic, creamy whole milk is sourced from pasture-raised cows and gently pasteurized to preserve its natural flavor and nutrients. Perfect for drinking, baking, or adding richness to your favorite recipes.

About Us

Milk Co. delivers the freshest and highest quality milk products from local farms to your table. Our commitment to excellence begins with the health and happiness of our cows, who graze on lush, pesticide-free pastures and are cared for by experienced farmers. We use modern, sustainable practices to ensure every bottle is pure, nutritious, and delicious. Our Farms

Our farms are the heart of Milk Co. With rolling green pastures and a tradition of stewardship, our farmers raise cows with care and respect. We believe that happy, healthy animals produce the best milk, so we invest in clean water, nutritious feed, and open grazing. Our team works closely with veterinarians and agricultural experts to maintain the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability. Many of our farms have been family-run for generations, passing down knowledge and a passion for quality.

Our Story

Milk Co. was founded in 1867 on the coastal plains near the mouth of the Zambezi River in what is now Mozambique. Joaquim Pereira, a small-scale dairy farmer, built a reputation for quality by combining traditional local husbandry with early cooling and handling techniques. From a single homestead herd, Joaquim and his neighbors formed a cooperative that focused on animal care, sensible breeding, and reliable delivery to nearby towns. Their dedication to quality and community quickly made Milk Co. a trusted name in the region.

Over the following decades, the cooperative gradually expanded its practices and reach—investing in pasteurization, cold-chain improvements, and agronomic training—while maintaining its ties to the land and local communities. By the mid-20th century, Milk Co. began exporting specialty dairy to neighboring regions and, eventually, to international markets. The company became known for its transparency, responsible farming, and the unique flavor of milk produced in Mozambique’s fertile valleys.

Today Milk Co. is a global brand that still sources from its original Mozambican farms and from carefully chosen partners around the world. The company blends heritage with modern sustainability: farmer education, pasture restoration, and humane animal care remain central. We invest in research, support local schools, and promote regenerative agriculture. From a modest 1867 homestead to an international presence, Milk Co. continues to bring traceable, thoughtfully produced dairy to tables worldwide, always honoring our roots and the people who make it possible.
